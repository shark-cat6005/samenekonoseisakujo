Pack Info

Automatic Item Refill Add-on

Ever run out of blocks while building and have to open your inventory mid-air?
Ran out of food during a fight and forgot to restock?
Or maybe your Totem of Undying just saved your life — but the next hit might be your last?

This add-on ensures your gameplay stays uninterrupted. It automatically refills essential items in your hotbar the moment they run out — smoothly and intelligently.

Main Features:
- Food auto-refill: keeps your preferred food item ready at all times
- Block auto-refill: instantly replaces depleted building blocks
- Totem auto-refill: automatically equips a new Totem of Undying from your inventory after one is used

Latest Version: v1.0.0
Compatible Minecraft Version: 1.21.71 and above
Experimental Features Required:
- Beta API (required)
